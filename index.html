A little bit about me.

I’ve made my home base in Tacoma, Washington with my small family. My journey into tech started with a job making informational web videos for state specific Social Securtiy sites. As my work began going live online, I was fascinated with how easily I could get myself and my vision out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build websites and apps.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’m working to make a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.
